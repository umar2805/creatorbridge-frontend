✦ Platform Features

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Built for real working relationships between creators and the talent who power their content.

Verified Professionals

Every editor and designer goes through a portfolio review and skill verification before joining. No guesswork — just qualified talent.

Portfolio Showcase

Creatives display their best work in rich visual portfolios. Browse real samples before sending a single message.

Direct Messaging

Real-time chat with file sharing and revision threads keeps everything in one place — no email chains, no missed context.

Secure Payments

Funds are held in escrow and released only when work is approved. Both sides are protected at every stage of the project.

↻ How It Works

From project brief to
final delivery in four steps.

1

Create Your Profile

Set up your creator or professional profile in minutes. Add your niche, style, and what you're looking for.

2

Post or Apply

Creators post projects with a brief and budget. Editors and designers browse and apply with a pitch and samples.

3

Connect & Collaborate

Chat, share files, align on vision. Use built-in revision tracking to stay organized from kickoff to delivery.

4

Complete & Get Paid

Creator approves the final deliverable, escrow releases automatically, and both parties leave verified reviews.
